# Marketplace Operations / 平台运营系统

Use this reference for daily operations, seller health, fulfillment, returns, customer service, and policy-risk sections.

用于每日运营、店铺健康分、履约、退货退款、客服和平台政策风险章节。

## Current Official-Source Watchlist / 当前官方核验重点

Check TikTok Shop Seller University before publishing final advice. Recent US seller pages emphasize:

- Account Health Rating (AHR): a 0-1,000 score reflecting seller activity over the last 180 days; new sellers start with 200 points. Policy violations and performance issues can deduct points.
- Seller enforcement: TikTok Shop may take additional actions as AHR drops to important thresholds such as 150, 100, 50, and 0 points.
- New shop probation: new sellers must treat early order handling, product accuracy, and policy compliance as a probation-period priority.
- Fulfillment performance: monitor OTDR, SFCR, unshipped order rate, out-of-stock cancellation rate, late shipment, and fulfillment defects.
- FBT: Fulfilled by TikTok can improve delivery speed, but fee cards, inbound rules, packaging rules, reimbursement rules, and incentive windows change. Do not hardcode a fee without checking the current FBT Rate Card.
- Returns/refunds: US return and refund workflows are policy-driven and should be handled inside Seller Center. Do not ask buyers to cancel or refund outside TikTok Shop processes.
- Customer service: keep buyer messages, cancellation, return, refund, and DNR handling inside platform-approved flows.
- Prohibited products and IP: check prohibited/restricted categories, claim rules, IP risk, counterfeit risk, and product documentation before listing.

发布最终建议前，先核对 TikTok Shop Seller University。近期美国卖家页面重点包括：

- Account Health Rating (AHR)：0-1,000 分，反映过去 180 天店铺活动；新卖家从 200 分开始。违规和履约问题会扣分。
- 卖家处罚：当 AHR 降到 150、100、50、0 等关键阈值时，TikTok Shop 可能采取进一步限制。
- 新店考核期：新卖家要把早期订单履约、商品准确性和政策合规当作优先级。
- 履约表现：监控 OTDR、SFCR、未发货率、缺货取消率、迟发和履约缺陷。
- FBT：Fulfilled by TikTok 可以提升配送速度，但费率、入仓规则、包装规则、赔付规则和激励期会变化；不要不查当前 FBT Rate Card 就写死费用。
- 退货退款：美国退货退款按平台政策流程处理，应在 Seller Center 内完成；不要引导买家绕过 TikTok Shop 流程取消或退款。
- 客服：买家消息、取消、退货、退款、DNR 都用平台允许的流程。
- 禁售品和 IP：上架前核对禁售/限售类目、宣称规则、IP 风险、仿牌风险和商品文件。

## Escalation SOP / 异常升级 SOP

1. Classify the issue: policy, fulfillment, product quality, customer service, IP, tariff/import, or inventory.
2. Stop the bleeding: pause risky listing, stop ad spend, hold inbound shipment, or update customer service script.
3. Save evidence: Seller Center screenshots, order IDs, tracking, product photos, supplier docs, broker emails.
4. Fix the root cause: listing copy, claims, packaging, QC, inventory buffer, creator brief, or supplier issue.
5. Record owner, deadline, and prevention rule.

1. 先分类：政策、履约、质量、客服、IP、关税进口或库存。
2. 先止血：暂停风险链接、暂停广告、暂停入仓或更新客服话术。
3. 留证据：后台截图、订单号、物流、产品照片、供应商文件、报关行邮件。
4. 修根因：链接文案、宣称、包装、质检、库存 buffer、达人 brief 或供应商问题。
5. 记录负责人、截止日期和预防规则。
