# Cross-Border Rules Snapshot / 跨境规则快照

Snapshot date: 2026-06-25. Tariffs and platform rules can change quickly. Use this file to structure the answer, then verify current official sources before giving final numbers.

快照日期：2026-06-25。关税和平台规则变化很快。用本文件组织判断，但最终数字必须核对当前官方来源。

## Current US Import Takeaways / 当前美国进口重点

- De minimis is not a reliable duty-free path. Duty-free de minimis treatment has been suspended globally for commercial low-value shipments. For non-postal shipments, entries are subject to applicable duties, taxes, fees, and charges. Postal shipments are dutiable under the current postal process until CBP implements a new entry process.
- 低值免税不能再当作免税路径。美国已对全球商业低值包裹暂停 duty-free de minimis。非邮政包裹要按适用税费报关；邮政包裹在 CBP 新流程生效前也要按当前邮政规则计税。
- IEEPA ad valorem duties under the China fentanyl and reciprocal tariff executive orders were ended by Executive Order 14389 on February 20, 2026. Do not keep adding the old China IEEPA 10% + reciprocal 10% as current charges unless official sources show they were reinstated.
- 2026 年 2 月 20 日 Executive Order 14389 已终止收取中国芬太尼/对等关税等 IEEPA 额外从价税。除非官方来源显示恢复，不要继续把旧的中国 IEEPA 10% + reciprocal 10% 当作当前税费加入。
- A temporary Section 122 import surcharge of 10% ad valorem applies to many imports from February 24, 2026 through July 24, 2026, unless the product is exempt, suspended, modified, terminated earlier, or extended by Congress.
- 2026 年 2 月 24 日至 2026 年 7 月 24 日，许多进口商品适用 Section 122 临时 10% 从价附加税；但要检查是否豁免、暂停、提前终止或由国会延长。
- Section 301 China tariffs still matter by HTS code. Some China-origin goods remain subject to Section 301 duties unless an active exclusion applies. USTR announced that 178 exclusions previously scheduled to expire on November 29, 2025 were extended until November 10, 2026; verify the current USTR/Federal Register notice and HTS Chapter 99 language before relying on an exclusion.
- Section 301 中国关税仍按 HTS 编码判断。部分中国原产商品仍有 Section 301 附加税；USTR 公告称原定 2025 年 11 月 29 日到期的 178 项排除延长至 2026 年 11 月 10 日；使用排除前必须核对当前 USTR/Federal Register 通知和 HTS Chapter 99 文字。
- Section 232, antidumping/countervailing duties, quotas, FDA/CPSC/FCC/FTC or other agency requirements can override simple margin math.
- Section 232、反倾销/反补贴、配额、FDA/CPSC/FCC/FTC 等监管要求，可能直接改变成本或能否销售。
- Treat every tariff statement as date-sensitive. Before quoting a final landed cost, check White House presidential actions, CBP CSMS/messages, USTR notices, and the current USITC HTS.
- 所有关税判断都要当作“按日期变化”。报价或下单前，必须核对 White House 行政令、CBP CSMS/公告、USTR 通知和 USITC 当前 HTS。

## Tariff Check Workflow / 关税核对流程

1. Identify the exact product, material, function, and end use.
2. Confirm country of origin, not just supplier location.
3. Find the 10-digit HTSUS code in the current USITC HTS.
4. Check base duty, Chapter 99 headings, Section 301 China list/exclusions, Section 122 surcharge, Section 232, AD/CVD, quotas, and agency restrictions.
5. Ask a customs broker to confirm before ordering inventory or promising a margin.
6. Put tariff assumptions in the SKU evaluation sheet and mark the date checked.

1. 明确产品、材质、功能和用途。
2. 确认原产国，不要只看供应商所在地。
3. 在 USITC 当前 HTS 中查 10 位 HTSUS 编码。
4. 核对基础税率、Chapter 99 附加税号、Section 301 中国清单/排除、Section 122 临时附加税、Section 232、AD/CVD、配额和监管限制。
5. 下库存订单或承诺利润前，让报关行确认。
6. 把关税假设写进 SKU 评估表，并记录核对日期。

## Shipment-Ready Verification / 出货前核验清单

Before placing a bulk order or shipping inventory to the US, require this evidence:

- Product description, material, function, intended use, photos, and spec sheet.
- Confirmed country of origin and supplier declaration.
- 10-digit HTSUS classification and basis for classification.
- Current base duty from USITC HTS.
- Current Chapter 99 and trade-remedy checks: Section 301, Section 122, Section 232, AD/CVD, quota, exclusions.
- De minimis and entry-type decision: do not assume duty-free entry.
- Customs broker confirmation with date, name, and email or document reference.
- Landed-cost worksheet showing margin after duty, freight, platform fees, refunds, creator/ads allowance, and fulfillment.

大货下单或发往美国前，要求留存这些证据：

- 产品描述、材质、功能、用途、图片和规格书。
- 原产国确认和供应商声明。
- 10 位 HTSUS 编码和归类依据。
- USITC HTS 当前基础税率。
- 当前 Chapter 99 和贸易救济检查：Section 301、Section 122、Section 232、AD/CVD、配额、排除。
- 低值免税和报关方式判断：不要默认免税入境。
- 报关行确认，记录日期、联系人和邮件或文件编号。
- 到岸成本表，包含关税、运费、平台费、退款损耗、达人/广告预留和履约成本后的利润。

## Landed Cost Formula / 到岸成本公式

Use this as a planning formula, not a legal customs calculation:

`landed cost = product cost + inland freight + international freight + insurance + customs duty + Section 301/122/232/AD/CVD if applicable + brokerage/entry fees + domestic fulfillment cost + return allowance + platform fees + affiliate/ads allowance`

规划时可用这个公式，不要当作法律报关计算：

`到岸成本 = 货品成本 + 国内段运费 + 国际运费 + 保险 + 基础关税 + 适用的 Section 301/122/232/AD/CVD + 报关/清关费用 + 美国本地履约成本 + 退货损耗预留 + 平台费用 + 达人/广告预留`

## Practical Seller Guidance / 卖家实操建议

- Do not choose SKUs with unclear HTS classification, high return risk, heavy weight, batteries, medical claims, children/baby safety claims, cosmetics/drugs, counterfeit/IP risk, or missing compliance documents.
- 不要优先做 HTS 编码不清、退货风险高、重量大、带电池、医疗宣称、婴童安全宣称、美妆药品属性、仿牌/IP 风险或缺少合规文件的 SKU。
- For China-origin goods, require a tariff worksheet before listing: HTS code, origin, base duty, Section 301 status, Section 122 status, other agency checks, broker name, check date, and margin after tariff.
- 中国原产商品上架前必须做关税表：HTS 编码、原产国、基础税率、Section 301 状态、Section 122 状态、其他监管、报关行、核对日期、含税后利润。
- If tariff impact is uncertain, test with low inventory and local fulfillment only after broker confirmation.
- 关税影响不确定时，先让报关行确认，再用低库存测试和美国本地履约。

## Official Source Checklist / 官方来源清单

- USITC HTS: current HTS code, base duty, Chapter 99 references.
- CBP CSMS and trade remedies pages: entry guidance, Section 122/301/232 filing order, AD/CVD, de minimis implementation.
- USTR Section 301 notices: China tariff lists and exclusions.
- White House presidential actions: new tariff proclamations, executive orders, suspension/termination dates.
- TikTok Shop Seller Center: marketplace fees, shipping/fulfillment SLA, returns/refunds, prohibited products, IP, account security.
