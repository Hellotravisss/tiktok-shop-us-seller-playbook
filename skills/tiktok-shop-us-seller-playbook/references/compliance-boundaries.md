# Compliance Boundaries / 合规边界

## Include / 可以包含

- Legitimate seller preparation: business documents, tax forms, bank/payment setup, warehouse and return address planning.
- 合法卖家准备：主体资料、税务资料、银行/收款、仓库和退货地址规划。
- Account security basics: unique email, password manager, two-factor authentication, role-based permissions, audit logs.
- 账号安全基础：独立邮箱、密码管理器、两步验证、角色权限、操作记录。
- Device and workflow hygiene: dedicated work phone/computer, stable office process, clean media library, clear owner for each account.
- 设备与流程管理：专用工作手机/电脑、稳定办公流程、干净素材库、账号责任人。
- US-market operations: product compliance checks, labeling, inventory accuracy, shipping promise, customer service, refunds, returns, reviews.
- 美国市场运营：商品合规、标签、库存准确性、发货承诺、客服、退款、退货和评价管理。
- Public official-source verification: ask the user to confirm current Seller Center screens and official policy pages before acting.
- 官方来源核对：执行前让用户确认当前 Seller Center 后台和官方政策页面。
- Legitimate cross-border alternatives: local US staff, authorized agency, local warehouse, official logistics providers, tax/accounting professionals.
- 合法跨境替代方案：美国本地员工、授权服务商、本地仓、官方物流服务商、税务/会计专业人士。

## Avoid / 不要包含

- VPN/proxy/node/airport configuration or recommendations.
- 代理、VPN、节点、机场配置或推荐。
- IP rotation, location spoofing, GPS spoofing, device fingerprint evasion, emulator bypass, anti-detection browser workflows.
- IP 轮换、定位伪装、GPS 伪装、设备指纹规避、模拟器绕过、反检测浏览器流程。
- Fake identity, fake documents, borrowed identity, bought/rented shops, account farms, or ways to bypass seller eligibility.
- 虚假身份、虚假资料、借用身份、买店租店、账号农场或绕过卖家资格的方法。
- Claims that guarantee approval, traffic, ranking, or revenue.
- 保证审核、流量、排名或收入的承诺。
- Shortcuts for restricted categories such as medical, supplement, finance, weapons, adult, counterfeit, or regulated products.
- 医疗、保健品、金融、武器、成人、仿牌或强监管品类的捷径。

## Redirect Pattern / 替代表述

English:

“I can’t help with bypassing platform or regional controls. I can help you build a compliant path: use real seller documents, assign authorized team roles, work with legitimate local partners where needed, and follow the current Seller Center requirements.”

中文：

“这部分我不能教规避平台或地区限制的做法。可以改成合规路线：使用真实资料，设置授权团队权限，必要时找合法本地合作方，并按 Seller Center 当前要求完成资料和运营。”
